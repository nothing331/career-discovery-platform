### Current idea (post-Phase-3, 2026-05-25)
- **Product:** B2B AI tool for Indian study-abroad counselors. Output is a take-home PDF report.
- **Buyer:** Mid-size Indian study-abroad agencies (5–50 counselors, 50–2,000 students/yr), AAERI+AIRC member or qualifying. Beachhead: Delhi NCR + Chandigarh + Punjab corridor.
- **Primary value framing (post-Phase-3 reframe):** PDF is a **trust-restoration artifact** the family takes home — addresses the corroborated post-payment trust collapse pain. The "save 2–3hrs" pitch is *secondary, founder-asserted, needs validation*.
- **Beachhead destinations:** Germany + Australia.
- **Source market:** India only at launch. Year-2 expansion: Nepal → Bangladesh → Sri Lanka → Kenya pilot. India-only ceiling ~₹1.5 Cr ARR.
- **Pricing tiers (locked in Phase 3):** ₹2,999 / ₹7,999 / ₹14,999 per month. Year-1 ARR target ~₹22.5L (25 agencies × ₹7,500 blended × 12).
- **Window:** 12–18 months before Meritto Mio AI ships a discovery-PDF bolt-on.
- **Team:** Unchanged — 2 SDEs, side project, ~10–15 hrs/week founder sales capacity.
- **Top action item:** Recruit 1 design-partner agency in next 30 days. The single highest-leverage gate condition.
